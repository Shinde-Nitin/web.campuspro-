Privacy Policy

CampusPro+ values your privacy. We only collect data that is necessary for the functioning of our services, such as student ID, course enrollment details, and academic records. All personal information is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third-party entities. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data. You can request access to your data or ask for its deletion by contacting our support team.
